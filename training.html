What to focus on

    Packing Guide

    Carry the essentials while staying light and prepared for mountain conditions.

    • Layered clothing, waterproof jacket, warm sleeping bag and quality boots.
    • Navigation tools, headlamp, water treatment, and spare batteries.
    • First aid kit, sunscreen, sunglasses, and compact emergency supplies.
    Practical skills

    Practice packing, map reading, and pacing before you leave.

    Beginner tips

    Start with shorter hikes and build up to longer, high-altitude days.
