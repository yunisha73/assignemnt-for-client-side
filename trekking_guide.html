Regions

Main trekking regions of Nepal

Different regions offer different trekking styles, cultural environments, and levels of remoteness.

Everest Region

Best known for major mountain views, Sherpa settlements, and iconic high-altitude trekking routes.

Annapurna Region

Known for varied landscapes, village life, long circuits, and a wide range of trekking experiences.

Langtang Region

A shorter and more accessible trekking area that suits many travelers with limited time.

Remote Routes

Manaslu, Dhaulagiri, and Makalu offer fewer crowds, more logistics, and a more demanding style.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Which trek is best for beginners?

Langtang Valley is a strong option because it is shorter, more accessible, and lower in altitude than many classic routes.

Do I need a guide in Nepal?

Some regions are better with a guide for safety and logistics. Restricted routes usually require more formal arrangements.

What is the best season for trekking?

Autumn and spring are the most popular because of clearer skies and better mountain conditions.

How should I prepare for altitude?

Ascend gradually, hydrate properly, rest when needed, and allow proper acclimatization on higher routes.
